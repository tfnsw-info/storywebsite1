How the water got to the plains - Additional Information

The story "How water got to the plains" is a story about how water got to the plains. It is part of the stories of the Butchalla people of K'Gari (Fraser Island).

\

The children thousands of years ago thus learnt that there was not always availability of water on mainland Australia as there is on K'Gari.
